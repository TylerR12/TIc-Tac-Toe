Instructions

The object of Tic Tac Toe is to get three in a row.

The first player is known as X and the second is O. Players alternate placing Xs and Os on the board until either player has three in a row or all nine squares are filled. In the event that neither player has three in a row and all squares have been filled, the round ends in a tie or also known as a cat game.
